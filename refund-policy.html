Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

At Impact Martial Athletics, we are committed to providing the highest quality youth martial arts and athletic training in Davie, Florida. We want every family to feel confident in their enrollment decision. Please read our refund policy carefully.

Trial Class Refunds

Our 50% off trial offer allows new students to experience our program at a reduced rate. Trial class fees are non-refundable once the student has attended the session. If you need to reschedule your trial class, please contact us at least 24 hours in advance at 954-636-1515 or tom@imaimpact.com.

Monthly Membership Refunds

Monthly membership fees are generally non-refundable. However, we may issue refunds or credits in the following circumstances:

  • Medical emergency or injury: If a student is unable to participate due to a medical condition documented by a physician, we will issue a prorated credit toward future classes.
  • Relocation: If a family relocates outside of the South Florida area (more than 30 miles from our location), we will issue a prorated refund for unused prepaid sessions.
  • Program cancellation: In the unlikely event that Impact Martial Athletics cancels a program or class series, enrolled students will receive a full refund for the cancelled sessions.

Cancellation Policy

To cancel or pause your membership:

  • Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing (email or in-person) at least 30 days before the next billing date.
  • Cancellations submitted less than 30 days before the billing date may be charged for the upcoming month.
  • To cancel, contact us at tom@imaimpact.com or call 954-636-1515.

Equipment and Merchandise

All sales on equipment, uniforms, gear, and merchandise are final. Exchanges may be offered for sizing issues within 14 days of purchase, provided items are unused, unwashed, and in original condition.

Summer Camp Refunds

Summer camp registrations are subject to the following refund schedule:

  • Cancellations made 30+ days before camp start: Full refund minus a $25 administrative fee.
  • Cancellations made 15–29 days before camp start: 50% refund.
  • Cancellations made less than 15 days before camp start: No refund. Credit toward future program may be offered at our discretion.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us:

  • Email: tom@imaimpact.com
  • Phone: 954-636-1515
  • In person: 4401 S Flamingo Rd, Davie, FL 33330

Please include your full name, the student's name, the reason for the refund request, and any supporting documentation. Refund requests are reviewed within 5-7 business days. Approved refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to make sure every family has a positive experience with Impact Martial Athletics.
